# Autonboard Demo Script
*For screen recording / Loom voiceover. ~75–80 seconds at natural speaking pace.*

---

**[Dashboard open, before starting a run]**

"If you've worked on a consumer product, you know onboarding sets the tone for everything downstream. A bad flow means fewer users ever reach the part of your product that makes them stay.

Analytics tools show you *where* the drop-off happens — but not *why*. User research tells you why, but it takes weeks to recruit, run, and synthesize.

This gets you those insights in minutes.

**[Start run, show agent stepping through]**

It's a browser agent. You point it at any product, tell it what a new user should accomplish, and it generates realistic user personas — different technical literacy levels, different motivations — and runs each one through your onboarding flow autonomously, in a real browser.

**[Show a friction moment — loop, cookie banner, confused click]**

At every step, the agent reasons out loud as that persona: 'I'm not sure what this button does,' 'I expected this to take me to my dashboard.' Every friction point is logged with a screenshot and a severity rating.

It also compares the PM's hypothesis about what the high-value first action should be against what the research actually suggests — so you're not just testing execution, you're pressure-testing the strategy.

**[Show final output / telemetry]**

At the end you get a structured report: what worked, where users got stuck, and what to fix — without scheduling a single session."
